2 Beat

Type: Stringing | Difficulty: Easy

A simple weave, with each poi doing one downbeat on opposite sides of the body.

Tips

    • • Try to keep the poi spinning parallel with your feet
      • As you get more comfortable, try to spin slower
      • It is encouraged to learn both the "left hand over" & "right hand over" variations (refer to video)
